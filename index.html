Welcome to Judah Wonders

Nursery & Primary Education in Abeokuta

Nestled in the heart of Abeokuta, behind Family FM on Abiola Way, Judah Wonders Model School is more than a school — it's a nurturing ground for tomorrow’s leaders. Founded by Mrs. Oluwabunmi Somoye, our institution is built on the pillars of justice, unity, distinction, achievement, and honesty.

We cater to Nursery and Primary pupils, offering a blend of spiritual development, sound qualitative academics, and moral instruction. Our highly motivated staff ensure every child is equipped for all‑round excellence.

With modern facilities, a secure learning environment, and a curriculum that meets global standards, Judah Wonders is where young minds bloom into generational wonders.

Our Core Values

The J‑U‑D‑A‑H Principles

J
Justice

Fairness and equity in all our dealings, teaching children to stand for what is right.

U
Unity

Fostering togetherness, collaboration, and a strong sense of community among pupils and staff.

D
Distinction

Excellence that sets us apart — in academics, character, and service.

A
Achievement

Celebrating every milestone and empowering each child to reach their highest potential.

H
Honesty

Integrity as a lifestyle, building trustworthy future leaders from the earliest years.
